    Foreword

    This tome contains all knowledge gathered about so-called "Legendary" artifacts,
    these objects although incredibly powerful under the right circumstances also bear a steep price.
    Be warned all who would seek these artifacts, some treasures are better of remaining lost.
    Should you still wish to proceed on your quest for these items, do know that not all locations are known at the time of writing,
    as such for the artifacts where the location is unknown any known information about it's whereabouts will be listed instead.

    Bag of Infinite Holding

    Location: Somewhere warm, the Azure-eyes will show the way.
    Value: 4.500 GP

    Said to be created by the great wizard Myrdin to solve hsi problem of never having the right book on-hand, this small satchel is said to be able to house any amount of objects without affecting it's appearance or weight.

    Carried goods no longer impact encumberance.

    Starfall Orb

    Location: There where the past ended.
    Value: 10.000 GP

    It's origin is unknown, it is rumored to have fallen from outer space in times long past. Even centuries later it is said to still hold the catalyst of the apocalypse.

    Crush it to call an asteroid from orbit towards an area of your choosing that you can see, after 2 turns it will arive. On impact causes 16D8 fire damage in a 150ft radius. Any coal in the blast radius is turned into diamonds however all other loot is destroyed.

    Enigmatic Box

    Location: In the museum of the elven monarch.
    Value: 8.000 GP

    Said to be a failed experiment of a goblin arch-mage, this small box contains all the magical energies it's creator once possessed.

    1/Day: Open the box to release a randomly chosen spell and experience the consequences.

    Scroll of Erasure

    Location: At the deepest point, guarded by those who dig.
    Value: 50.000 GP

    Ancient legends speak of this scroll said to contain the essence of death itself, it is said that whoever reads the scroll instantly ceases to exist.

    Reading the scroll causes the reader to instantly die and cease to exist along with all their loot and the scroll itself.

    Bracelet of Perilous Gamble

    Location: Somewhere really hot, next to the biggest yellow rock.
    Value: 12.000 GP

    Rumored to be the result of a poorly worded wish by a arrogant mage, this bracelet is said to make the wearer survive anything... for a short time.

    1/Day: Twist the bracelet to guarantee that you survive the next damage you take, instead the next damage causes your health to become 1 and after 2 turns you will take 15 (unavoidable) damage.

    Club of Unrestrained Rage

    Location: Held by a infamous orc warlord in the north.
    Value: 6.500 GP

    Created by an ancient shaman for his chieftan, this club looking like a wooden branch with red runes carved into it is said to greatly enhance the wielder's hits, if they can wield it without falling over that is.

    A standard +2 wooden club with a 10+ crit range, however whenever you succesfully score a hit you must succeed a 16+ CON save or be knocked prone.

    Prismatic Blade

    Location: In a hidden grove of the undine kingdom.
    Value: 21.000 GP

    Said to be once used by Gaia in a fierce battle against Tiamat, this blade is imbued with the power of the elements.

    A standard +2 greatsword, whenever an attack with this weapon is made against a target the damage automatically becomes the element the target is weakest against.

    Torrent Bottle

    Location: In the wreck of the S.S. Vengeful Cutlass.
    Value: 5.500 GP

    Through unknown means and for unknown reasons an ancient wizard managed to fit an entire ocean into a small bottle.

    Break the bottle to create a rapidly expanding amount of water(200ftÂ²) on impact.

    Needle of Entwined Destiny

    Location: In the caldera of the cauldron.
    Value: 28.000 GP

    Rumored to be created by Pharasma, this small but ornate needle is said to entwine the destinies of whoever comes into contact with it.

    2/Day: Prick a target with the needle(+3 To Hit) to link their destiny with the second target, whatever happens to the one also happens to the other(damage, status effects, conditions, etc.).

    Duskfell Cloak

    Location: There where the bell tolls for those with a bird's eye view.
    Value: 17.500 GP

    Said to be woven from the hair of a shadow-bound demon, this cloak renders it's wearer completely unfindable but vunerable.

    Touch the clasp to become perfectly hidden but unable to attack, touch again to dispell, once dispelled for the next 2 turns the all succesfull hits against the wearer are considered crits.

    Bracers of the Inevitable

    Location: Buried with the last king of the giants.
    Value: 1.900 GP

    Crafted for the king of the giants by an talented and cunning dwarven blacksmith, these bracers are said to make combat shorter, somehow.

    While wearing the bracers all attacks by and against the wearer will hit their target if possible(ignore AC & TH).

    Ring of Avarice

    Location: On the bottom of a lake where a great ancient battle took place.
    Value: 16.500 GP

    Said to be created in a deal with the devil, this ring can grant unlimited wealth or take it all away.

    Whenever you touch the ring to your lips 10.000GP will be created out of thin air, then a D20 will be rolled, on a 20 the user will lose every posession they have to their name.

    Scroll of Wish

    Location: In the dragon kingdom, in THE chest.
    Value: 250.000 GP

    It is said that if read aloud, the scroll will summon a genie to fullfill a single wish.

    On use can ask DM for a single sentence effect of choice as long as it doesn't ruin/break the campaign, interpretation of the wording used is up to the DM.

    Wreath of Harmony

    Location: Bestowed upon the emissary of the great spore.
    Value: 7.200 GP

    Created by the great spore, this wreath made of mushrooms spreads the spore's message of peace and serenity to all.

    Both the wearer and every being within 100ft. become incapable of harming anyone, enemies in range also become very friendly until taken off.
